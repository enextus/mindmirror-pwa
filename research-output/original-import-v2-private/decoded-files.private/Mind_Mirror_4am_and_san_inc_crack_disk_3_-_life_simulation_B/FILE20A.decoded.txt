HIGH CHAIR POLITICS
Momentous Decisions! Will you accept solid foods? How do you handle the perils of crawling and walking?

You're lying warm and cozy in your crib. Suddenly Mommy lifts you up and carries you bump-bump-bump to the kitchen. She slips you in a HIIIIIIIIGH chair. This place is very jangly. Noise! TV blaring. Dishwasher hiss and clang. Dad, at the table, clashes silverware. Mommy comes over and shoves a syrupy mush into your mouth-piece with a metal shovel!

Grosseroo! Kinky-poo! You're used to natural foods. The soft, warm Noobler-Brand Stuff that you can suck, muck and slurp and burp. Well, let's go along with the gag (gulp).

THE BIRTHDAY PARTY
You enter the mad social world of a 4-year old. A birthday party, crowded with potential friends and enemies!

At four, you've reached the pinnacle of courageous exploration and external achievement.

And here's Dadster, himself, carrying your toggers. Apparently you're off to a kid's birthday party. You're led into a room filled with kids.

Some big, noisy girls are wrestling and shouting. Some of the boys are, too. Balloons. Streamers. You stand there stupidly holding the present. It's for a kid named Ronnie.
