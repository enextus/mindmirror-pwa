EXERCISE 2. PAST LOVES

Do you dare look back at the loves of yesterday? Do you agree with Arthur Symons, who lamented, "I cannot, having been your lover, stoop to being your friend?"

Do you sigh when Byron whispers, "The fruits and flowers of love are gone; the worm, the canker, and the grief are mine alone!" Let's perform a quick scan of your lurid past.

1. YOUR FIRST CRUSH: Think of the first person on whom you had a steamy crush. Don't be afraid of going weak at the knees!
