PSYCHOLOGICAL CONSULTANT
1
MIND MIRROR can be a valuable tool in psychological work.
1. PSYCHO-DIAGNOSIS: Staff members can compare their views of a client.
2. INTERACTION: The client and you can compare thoughts.
3. CLIENT INSIGHT: Teach the client to Micro-Scope his/her views of self, history, significant others.
4. PROGRESS: Staff and client plot changes in course of counseling.
5. GROUP ENCOUNTERS: Group members compare perceptions.
6. SIMULATIONS: Put yourself and your client in a variety of roles. This can lead to valuable insights.

PERSONNEL MANAGEMENT CONSULTANT
1
MIND MIRROR increases understanding of interpersonal relations. Personnel Management Consultants use it to assess:

1. JOB CHARACTERISTICS: Mind-Scope requirements for each job in a company.
2. STAFF APPRAISAL: Use Mind Mirror to compare employees/applicants with jobs.
3. STAFF INSIGHT: Employees and applicants compare selves with jobs.
4. COMPANY DYNAMICS: All employees scope views of relevant others.
5. DECISION MAKING: Plot your game moves.
6. MARKETING: Scope target-groups and compare with products.

EDUCATIONAL CONSULTANT
1
The Electronic MIND MIRROR is a Thought-Making-Tool. Education usually consists of passing heavy "Thought-Stones" from generation to generation. These Macro-thoughts can be used to crush new ideas.

Now students can clarify and map their own thoughts about history, philosophy, science, politics, culture, themselves. Students' ideas can be plotted and compared with those of the teacher or other students. Learning becomes active, creative, inventive. The aim of the teacher in the Information Society is to help students THINK FOR THEMSELVES with precision and clarity.
